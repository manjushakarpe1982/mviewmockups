Plain-English glossary for this report

  • Mcf — a thousand cubic feet of natural gas. The standard unit on royalty statements.
  • Royalty interest — your percentage share of what a well produces, paid without you paying drilling or operating costs.
  • TTM — trailing twelve months: the last 12 reported months added together.
  • Decline — every well produces less over time; a gentle slope is normal and expected.

Sources & method. Production: Texas Railroad Commission lease-cycle filings, complete through April 2026 (operators file ~2–3 months behind). Ownership percentages: claimed interests as recorded with Mineral View. Valuations: Mineral View MVestimate model, updated July 6, 2026. Permit/completion activity: RRC filings, 2026 year-to-date.
